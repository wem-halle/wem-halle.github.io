Directions

The workshop will take place at the Faculty of Economic Sciences, Große Steinstraße 73, 06108 Halle (Saale) in room SR 1. The tram stop Juliot Curie Platz is directly in front of the of the faculty building. Both tram lines 2 & 5 stop here. The building is only a 5 minute walk from Marktplatz, where most trams stop. However, the easiest way to get around beautiful Halle is to walk.
